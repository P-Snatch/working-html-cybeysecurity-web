What is Cybersecurity?

Cyber security is a how we can protect and defend digital systems and data from unauthorized access. It uses defence
such as Firewalls. monitoring and encyption to prevent cyber threats and attacks. the aim is to ensure data
private, integrity, availability while reduces overall risk Cyber security is a constant growing area
of IoT (internet of things). It requires prevention, detection, risk asessment, incident response, compliance and eductaion.
Cybersecurity needs to constantly evolve to keep up with new threats to keep our data safe.


This website will go in to detail on 5 subsections of cyber security and breiefly explain the vital roles they play to
keep our information safe from threats such as hackers, malware and data loss. The five topics that we will go over are:
- Network Security
- Application Security
- Data Security
- Incidents response and Management
- Identity and access Management (IAM)
These sections can be seen on the side of the page and will each take you to information about how they do their job effectively.
